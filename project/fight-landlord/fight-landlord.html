Fight the LandlordCard Game

Fight the Landlord is a card game. I was a game developer and IT assistant at Beijing AINONG Nursing Home Service Co. Ltd. My main job was to develop this card game for elders in the nursing home to play on PC.

Considering that my target players are elders, I created larger UI for them to see better, I added voice lines to make the experience better, also I made the opponent AI not too "smart", in order to improve player experience.
