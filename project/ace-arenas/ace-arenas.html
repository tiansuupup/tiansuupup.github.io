Ace of ArenasMobile MOBA

Ace of Arenas was a mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Gaea Mobile Limited. There were over 1,000,000 players from around the world. The game receives rating above 4.5/5 on multiple game platforms.

I was a part-time game designer and official content creator, working for the China Headquarters, also know as "盖亚互娱-自由之战". The most significant part of my work was that I was participated in the design of the Gem System, which is the core monetization system of the game.

Fun fact: I was invited to the team because I was one of the top 0.1% players of the game, and I have experience and strong interest in game development.
