Everywhere At the End of TimeSerious Game

Everywhere At The End of Time is a 3D First Person Serious game. This is a school project for a course of the Northeastern University GSND program.

The game is made in a team, and I was the Lead Programmer and Lead Designer.

The game is about Elin, who was an Alzheimer's patient, slowly remembered lot of things about her life one day.

The game aims to raise players' attentions to Alzheimer's patients.
