Unusual NightPlatformer

Unusual Night (无常夜岚), a mobile game, is a team project for Tencent Minigame Project. The Tencent Minigame Project is an apprenticeship opportunity for qualified participants to develop game projects with the guidance of Tencent professional game designers, and there is a final evaluation for Tencent to decide whether to buy the game idea and continue to work on it. Some previous award-winning games are 三竹里, 泥山萨满，曲中剑. This game won the Silver Awards, which means it is the second best game among all the games in the final competition. I was the lead programmer and the lead game designer of the team.
The game is about a story in ancient China, 3 kings fight against each other and treat their people badly. 3 soldiers from these 3 different countries, at the beginning believed their own king is the only justice in the world, slowly discovered the darkness of the countries, the kings and the war.
