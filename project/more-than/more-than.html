More Than Meets the EyeSerious Game

More Than Meets The Eye is a 2D Serious Adventure Game, made with Unity. The game is about people's inside part that they do not show others. This is a team project we made for the Global Game Jam, 2022 Boston.

I designed and write the dialogue system for the game. I also participated in the story writing and helped other programmers.

The story is about the main character accidently got the power to read other's mind. He discovered that everyone has hidden thoughts behind their actions, and player could choose whether to use this ability to help others or not.
