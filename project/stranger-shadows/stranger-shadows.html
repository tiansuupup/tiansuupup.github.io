Stranger ShadowsHorror

Stranger Shadows is a 3D Horror Game, involving the use of eye-tracking technology. This was a school project. I was the lead designer and lead programmer of the game, other team members and I together wrote a Thesis Paper based on the player data we gathered. The use of eye-tracking technology is used to create a completely new type of player experience. The enemies and puzzles are designed for eye tracker. Player uses the eye-tracking webcam to control the flashlight in the game, and this flashlight is the key to all the game mechanics.
