Zombie LabHorror FPS

Zombie Lab is a First Person Shooter game. I designed this game because I used to play games like Cross Fire, CS Online, and Call of Duty: Black Ops 4, and I was really interested in their zombie mode. I created this project in Unity.

The game is about a evil company is planning to spread zombie virus through satellite, and the only way to stop this from happening is to use the particle cannon in the secret lab. However, in order to do that, player need to collect bio-batteries and turn on the generators. And, of course, there are zombies everywhere in the building.
