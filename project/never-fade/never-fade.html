Never Fade AwayFPS + Stealth

Never Fade Away is a 3D FPS stealth game. The game is about a hero preventing an Evil AI trying take over human beings, by uploading a virus to the AI's main server, which is located on an island, then retreat from the island.

The project is a team project. I am the Lead Programmer, Lead Designer & Modeler of this project.

Some specific techniques invloved are:
IK, Behavior-Tree and Maya, Substance Painter.
