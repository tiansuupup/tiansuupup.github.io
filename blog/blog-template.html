2 Minutes 16 Seconds is a Rogue-Lite 2D Flying Shooter game. The game is inspired by the show, Battlestar Galactica. Every 2 minutes and 16 seconds, the enemy mothership track down the player mothership and try to eliminate the player. The player need to collect fuel and different upgrades, while fighting against small alien spaceships in order to survive. The player can JUMP at anytime, if the fuel is sufficient. The interesting part of the game is the Rogue Element. Each time player could get random weapons or upgrades from power-ups, and player could build up completely different yet interesting composition based on them.
